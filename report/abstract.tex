  \begin{abstract}
    \noindent The military of ancient Rome is unanimously considered a key element in the rise of Rome. We developed a visual analytics tool for educational purposes, mainly to give a visual representation of ancient Roman battles and wars. The multiple interactive views of our project present high level features, but the user also has the possibility to explore further information, e.g. via an info-box popup associated to each battle.
  \end{abstract}
